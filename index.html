The problem we saw

The "leaders of tomorrow" lack a comprehensive understanding and interest in how their nation's and Africa's government systems function. Many of them believe they have no meaningful role to play within these systems. Additionally, it has become evident that the average young person is highly susceptible to the overwhelming amount of information flooding social media platforms. This has led to a skewed perspective among young people, making them vulnerable to misinformation. Furthermore, most young individuals lack proper guidance and mentorship within the public service space, which is crucial for their development and potential as future leaders.
