TOF NATION


Teens on fire leadership network, is a teen centeric leadership community that is dedicated to nurturing, grooming, and training the next generations of African leaders. We are aware of the importance and need for strong institutions in Africa that are headed by qualified, skilled, and intelligent leaders.

At TOFNation, we aim to erase misconceptions about young people and leadership by promoting leadership with strong emphasis on public service thereby cultivating informed and interested youth in governance. "Our goal is to be Africa's leading community thats provide credible information on leadership, governance, policy-making, and politics to empower young individuals and inspire their active participation in becoming informed, ethical, and engaged leaders as we believe that teenagers are not too young to lead".
